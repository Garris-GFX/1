Hello! Below you will find the full breakdown of the work I did for the concept: Fueco Pizzeria. Disclaimer: This is not a paid client project. Some areas may be underdeveloped or need more work. Thank you for taking a look!
## The Challenge
To create a recognizable brand that brings the heat. Designing packaging, menus, and marketing materials that reinforce the handcrafted, wood-fired experience. Establishing a strong digital presence, including social media visuals and a logo that feel cohesive and high-end.
## Logo Design
Using retro typography, the shape of a classic pizza oven, and flame inspiration, I created this logo family for this concept to make its mark.
![The brand needed a capstone for thumbnails. By combining the fiery red that the brand uses to attract attention, set against a light tan for the main typography, it's subtle but warm.](/work/fueco-pizza/1.png)
![This image features the logo variations Fueco could use for everything from packaging to social media. These designs unite to create a comprehensive logo family.](/work/fueco-pizza/2.png)
## Color Palette
Fueco’s colors capture the heat and intensity of wood-fired pizza. Fiery Red and Ember evoke flames and passion, while Charcoal adds depth and smokiness. Ivory provides balance, ensuring clarity and sophistication. This palette reinforces Fueco’s bold, high-energy identity at every touchpoint.
![In a stroke of creativity, matches were chosen to illustrate the brand's chosen color system. The flames are energetic and connect to the ethos of fire-baked pizza.](/work/fueco-pizza/3.png)
## Menu & Wine Packaging
For this section, I wanted to really give the viewer a look into what it would be like to sit and sip a glass of wine at this cozy, Italian pizzeria. I used Photoshop to add in the menu design I created for the brand and added a subtle grain to give it an "old world" quality. This style can be reused for all the brand imagery.
![In this image you will find a table setting that gives the brand a warm and homey feel. This was intentional. Adding the menu and wine list brought the idea together. This is what it would be enjoying your time at Fueco.](/work/fueco-pizza/4.png)
![In this mock-up two wine bottles dance together on a deep red background. For the label design, I kept it simple using the dark background and linework to create a striking contrast.](/work/fueco-pizza/5.png)
## Social Media
Fueco’s social media design embraces high-contrast visuals, bold typography, and dynamic layouts to capture attention instantly. The mix of mouthwatering food photography, strong brand elements, and vibrant red accents ensures a cohesive, scroll-stopping presence. Every post reinforces Fueco’s fiery, fast-paced energy, making the brand impossible to ignore.
![Alternating panels combine to provide a trendy look into the many working parts of a pizzeria. Elements of typography, custom patterns, product and lifestyle photography, and more give an energetic and cohesive vibe to their social media.](/work/fueco-pizza/6.png)
![In this picture, Instagram post mock-ups illustrate snapshots of product photography, logo design, packaging design, and lifestyle imagery.](/work/fueco-pizza/8.png)
## Basic Website/Application Design
Although I didn't expand on this section of the project as much as I would have liked, I used a laptop outline to throw together a quick look at what the brand's website or pizza order app could look like. Emphasis on simple line art that adds structure without distracting from the main message: DELICIOUS PIZZA!
![I sourced some mouthwatering product photography for this section, and I think it brought the heat. Product photography is a must for any food/beverage branding.](/work/fueco-pizza/7.png)
