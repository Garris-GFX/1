Thanks for checking out this project! Find out more about the work I did for Elite One Financial below.
## The Inspiration
This client approached me wanting something very clean that matched the typography-forward inspiration they had picked for their logo. I love when clients have an idea of how they want their branding to look, and when it aligns with their industry, it checks out. We explored some typography and simple ways to make it cool.
## Logo Design
After testing some gradient options and making some tweaks, we landed on this simple but modern logo. Below you can see it illustrated with different configurations the client could use for multiple applications.
