Hello! As the very first project in this catalogue, it is a special pleasure to introduce you to the work completed for Murray Marketing. Please scroll to explore.
## Website Mock Up
The heart of Murray Marketing lives online. To connect brands together, their website is a crucial point in their infrastructure. This mock-up shows what that could look like, featuring their new logo design and color system.
![In this image, a rudimentary impression of the brand's website can be seen presented in an abstract way.](/work/murray-marketing/1.png)
## Digital Marketing Cards
For these mock-ups, I focused on showing range without breaking consistency. The navy and gold palette carries authority, while the layout variations keep things modern and adaptable. This approach lets the brand live comfortably across websites, ads, and campaigns without feeling repetitive or boxed in.
![A set of modular digital card designs showing flexible layout options for web and social use. Each card explores hierarchy, movement, and content balance within the Murray Marketing brand.](/work/murray-marketing/2.png)
## Business Card Mock Up
The deep navy sets a professional tone, while the gold accent adds just enough edge without feeling flashy. I wanted it to feel solid in someone’s hand.
![A clean, in-hand business card mock-up emphasizing scale, contrast, and legibility.
The logo and color banding are designed to read instantly in a real-world setting.](/work/murray-marketing/3.png)
## Mobile Device Mock Up
I chose this mock-up to show how the brand translates beyond print. The gold backdrop keeps the screen bold and recognizable, while the centered mark keeps things calm and intentional.
![A branded mobile screen showing the Murray Marketing identity applied in a digital environment. The device styling ties directly into the brand’s core color system.](/work/murray-marketing/4.png)
## Color System
The client wanted colors that looked great and added class. The gold brings warmth and approachability, while the navy grounds the brand with trust and professionalism. Including full color values ensures the brand stays accurate no matter where it’s used.
![A full brand color breakdown showing primary colors with CMYK and HEX values. This palette anchors both digital and print production.](/work/murray-marketing/5.png)
## Thumbnail
The client needed a thumbnail that looks confident and brings focus to their new logo. The gold gradients add sophistication and a touch of elevation.
