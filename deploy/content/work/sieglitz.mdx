## Business Card
When the owner approached me about helping with their project, I was very excited to get started on a new logo we could build the rest of the marketing materials and branding from. We put together a logo that worked for their industry and played with the brand typography in a way that fit the client’s vision for the brand. One thing that elevated this design compared to the others we tested was the way the drone logo mark is interacting with the "T" in Sieglitz. This playful nod to drone work helped carry the mission and reinforce the brand.
