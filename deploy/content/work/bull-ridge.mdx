Bull Ridge isn’t your average insurance agency. Built on trust, strength, and a no-BS approach, we make coverage simple, clear, and reliable. No jargon, no confusion—just real protection for what matters most.
## The Problem
Insurance is overwhelming. Too many options, too much fine print, and too little transparency. People want coverage they can understand and an agency they can actually trust.
![This image features the company logo with a neat perspective in an office setting. High-quality mock-ups are helpful for the designer and client to test their new logo in the real world.](/work/bull-ridge/1.png)
## Brand Color Palette
Every color is intentional. Electric Blue (#2642FF): high-energy and unmistakable. Deep Navy (#010333): strong, steady, reliable. Phantom Gray (#DBDBDB): clean, modern, and keeps the focus where it belongs. No fluff—just a brand built to last.
![Color system used to create the new branding for Bull Ridge Insurance Agency.](/work/bull-ridge/2.png)
## Social Media & Online Presence
Consistency is key. The sharp angles and bold contrasts make Bull Ridge stand out across platforms. The blue is strong, the layout is clean, and the brand stays instantly recognizable.
![Custom profile pictures and a good favicon are essential for a redesign. Above you will find the new profile picture and favicon I designed for Bull Ridge.](/work/bull-ridge/3.png)
## Weekly Zoom Thumbnail
A bold, structured backdrop designed for impact. The grid represents precision, while the mirrored typography adds depth. Electric blue keeps it sharp and modern—no clutter, just clear communication.
![In this mock-up you see the brand assets and logo work together on one of the brand's seminar templates. Although this isn't their real space, it gave us a good idea of what their designs would look like in a seminar setting.](/work/bull-ridge/4.png)
## Business Card Design
Sleek, no-nonsense, and built for a lasting impression. Deep navy for professionalism, electric blue for energy, and a solid layout that gets straight to the point.
![This business card design keeps the brand's logo as the main focus. Adding a pop of electric blue with the logo mark in the center. Social media links and the website/email are also included on the front of the card.](/work/bull-ridge/5.png)
## Branded Backgrounds & Wallpapers
The client needed professional, branded Zoom screens for meetings and webinars. The deep blue tones and geometric patterns keep the look sharp and modern, while the subtle logo placement reinforces brand presence without distraction.
![In this image, two sets of matching Zoom screens give the brand options when it comes to connecting with members of their insurance agency staff or with their clients. These screens pop in as the background for conference calls or as company desktop wallpapers.](/work/bull-ridge/6.png)
## Thank You Letter Design
Not just words—an experience. The repeating “THANK YOU” pattern reinforces appreciation without feeling generic. The grid keeps things structured, just like the agency’s approach.
![Displayed is a stylized thank-you card using a modern grid asset, dark gradients, and the snappy horizontal version of the client's logo.](/work/bull-ridge/7.png)
## Email Signature
Professional but personal. A bold name, clean layout, and sharp contrast make sure every email looks polished. The background pattern ties everything back to the brand.
![Lastly, the client requested matching email signatures they can use for their business email accounts. This image features the brand's custom email signature.](/work/bull-ridge/8.png)
