Below you will find more information about the work I completed for Carolina Portable Buildings. Special thanks to them for including me in this project. Scroll to explore.
## The Inspiration
After visiting the client at their business location and checking out their selection of buildings, I began some quick sketches. Oftentimes this is an easy way to capture some of the shapes that could be helpful in creating a logo. In this case, it was barn doors, transom windows, dormers, etc.
## Logo Design
After drawing up several different portable building designs, we landed on this three-point perspective version, and the client was satisfied. We chose a popular, easy-to-read typography for their logo mark.
