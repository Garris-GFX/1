Small design choices add up. Professional brands feel intentional because their pieces fit together.

This article walks through the practical things that make a brand feel put-together and trustworthy.

## Typography

Consistency in typography matters more than clever fonts. Use a small, well-scaled system and stick to it.

## Spacing

Good spacing creates rhythm. It helps users parse groups of content quickly.

## Photography

Use real photos when possible — stock shots often feel generic.
